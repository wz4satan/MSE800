
## Improvements
### The First One
The use cases `Handle Returns` and `Cash In` are extensions of the general `Process Sale` and `Handle Returns` workflow. They are not the whole of the workflow, for example, nowadays, money transferring is more than `cash in`.

We could draw four `extend` arrows for these four use cases; both `Handle Returns` and `Cash In` have two `extend` arrows starting from them toward both `Process Sale` and `Process Rental`.

### The Second One
Deleting the actor `HR System` and the line between it and use case `Cash In` should be good for the most really business activities. No legitimate and formal institution will allow `HR System` directly participate in the currency cycle without `Accounting System`.
